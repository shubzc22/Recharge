Privacy Policy

1. Introduction: Nira Studio & Media values your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you provide while participating in our photography contest or using our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information Collection: We may collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and payment details when you register for the contest. Additionally, we may collect photographs and related metadata (EXIF) submitted as contest entries.

3. Use of Information: The information collected is used solely for contest administration, communication regarding contest updates, prize distribution, and for verifying the authenticity of entries. We may also use data for promotional purposes related to the contest, including displaying winners and their work on our website or social media.

4. Data Sharing: Personal information will not be shared with third parties except for purposes related to contest management, such as courier services for sending certificates or prizes, or if required by law. We do not sell or trade your information.

5. Cookies and Tracking: Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience, track registration activity, and improve our services. No sensitive personal data is collected through cookies.

6. Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security due to the inherent risks of online data transmission.

7. Access and Control: Participants have the right to access, update, or request deletion of their personal information by contacting us through official communication channels. We will make reasonable efforts to accommodate such requests in compliance with applicable laws.

8. Consent: By participating in the contest or using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. You also consent to the display of your submitted photographs for promotional purposes related to the contest.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy: Nira Studio & Media reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Participants will be informed of changes through our official website or communication channels. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us: For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at contact@nirasm.com or through our official website.
